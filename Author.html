CALEB HUI


I have loved to draw since an early age and taught myself the basics of drawing. Because of ADHD, I have some difficulty focusing. In class I frequently drew comics because I found class boring, much to the annoyance of my school teachers. I started drawing when I was at least 5-6 years old, and I wanted to draw because I read books and watched videos, and wanted to draw the things I watched.
What inspired you to become a writer?
Reading books and watching cartoons as a kid.
What genres do you like to write in?
Mainly comedy-action, i always like to laugh.
Are there any specific themes or messages you like to explore in your writing?
Not much.
What are your aspirations as a writer?
I want to bring joy to others and make them laugh.
Do you have any upcoming projects or books you’re working on?
More short comics about the nostalgia gang getting into hilarious incidents, probably Green vs AI part 2.
What do you want readers to know about you?
I am just some guy who draws and writes for fun.
Is there a fun fact or unique detail about yourself that you would like to share?
I’m just a pretty average guy, to be honest. I love reading, cartooning, but the special thing about me is that I have ADHD.

Behind the scenes

  • Ideate

    I want to create stories for an audience of friends, parents, and comic lovers ages 8-13. My trashy website aims to share my (badly drawn) but awesome comics as part of my course project. I incorporated my favorite color (Green, Green, and more Green), stupid themes, and user-friendly navigation to engage visitors.

  • Design

    I started building this website with simple (and very bad) sketches (wireframe) on paper. We then made the user interface on Figma

  • Evaluate

    This website allows me to show people my comics that I have drawn easily because I have lost some of my best works at home. More people can now see my comics since my website is now on github, and I can upload comics ever so often.
  • Develop

    After doing the wireframe and making the user interface. Then, I started coding the longest code i ever did in my entire history at preface. I made this website with HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
